Who We Are

YWAG is a national platform created to support and promote young and emerging writers across The Gambia. We aim to inspire creativity, develop talent, and build a community of literary thinkers.

Founded in 2012, our association has grown to become the leading voice for young writers in the country, with members from all regions participating in our programs and initiatives.

Our Mission

To empower young Gambian writers through education, mentorship, and publication opportunities while preserving and promoting our rich literary heritage.

We achieve this through workshops, competitions, networking events, and partnerships with educational institutions and cultural organizations.

Submit Your Writing

We are always looking for new voices. Send us your writing : fiction, poetry, short essays, reviews, or commentary.

Submission Guidelines

  • Only original, unpublished work.
  • No AI generated works
  • Simultaneous submissions allowed; inform us if accepted elsewhere.
  • Suggested word limits:
    • Fiction: ≤ 2,500 words
    • Book Review / Essay: ≤ 1,500 words
  • Include a short bio and optional author photo.
  • We only send acceptance notices; refrain from reposting.
